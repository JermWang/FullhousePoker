Fullhouse is the reskin of Velvet from a cold velvet vault into a packed, neon-lit card house. Deep-plum night, electric magenta and amber, glowing teal felt — the same restrained product, now buzzing and alive. Every gameplay object keeps its exact size; only the skin changes.

01 · The mark

From an engraved “V” to a glowing FH chip

Velvet's quiet monogram becomes a chip you'd actually want to stack: an amber FH on black marble, ringed in magenta and teal neon. It reads at 20px in the nav and at 280px on the felt.
